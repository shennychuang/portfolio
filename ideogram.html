Ideogram Design














This is an ideogram design symbolized the Syrian war. Based on the research, in 2014, a total of 219 thousand refugees were rescued as they attempted to cross the Mediterranean; however, there's an additional 400 refugees drowned while crossing the sea. The appearance of the ideogram shows a Syrian woman and a child, and the fold on the khimar (headscarf) is showing as the shape of the wave. Under the ideogram is a pattern design recreated from the ideogram.
